Dear Members,

Are you aiming for a postdoctoral research fellowship in the U.S.? Don’t miss out on our upcoming open webinar, **‘Navigating Postdoctoral Research Fellowships in the U.S.,’** presented by RSAP’s Mentorship & Education Committee!

Mark your calendars for **August 18th, 2024, at 8:30 PM Pakistan Time (PKT)** and join us live on **Zoom**. This exclusive webinar will feature three distinguished panelists who are current postdoctoral research fellows at top U.S. universities, including Ivy League institutions. They will provide invaluable insights into the entire process, from understanding the application requirements and securing funding to finding the best opportunities and sharing real-life experiences as a research fellow.

Whether you're a medical student or graduate, this webinar is **open to everyone**, including non-members. This is a great opportunity to learn directly from experts who have successfully navigated this journey and can provide the guidance you need to do the same.
