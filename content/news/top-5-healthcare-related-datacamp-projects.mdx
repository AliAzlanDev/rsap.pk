Dear Members,

We are excited to announce that applications for the 2nd batch of the **RSAP x DataCamp Donates Scholarship** are open. This is an incredible opportunity for our members to enhance their research and data science expertise, absolutely free!

RSAP stands out as one of Pakistan's pioneering student and trainee-led organizations offering this unparalleled opportunity. With the ongoing success of the first batch, we've achieved increased slots and are now looking to provide the scholarship to more deserving recipients!

### Why You Should Apply?
As an **RSAP x DataCamp Donates Scholarship** recipient, you will receive a **full one-year subscription to DataCamp**, providing unlimited access to 400+ expertly designed courses and 110+ hands-on projects. This package is valued at over **$399**—and it's all yours, **free of charge**!

### Why use DataCamp as a medical student or doctor?
In today's healthcare landscape, advanced technology, data analytics, and machine learning algorithms are integral to modern radiology and other medical fields. Big data plays a crucial role in tasks such as tumor detection, disease modeling, and personalized treatment plans. Mastering these advanced techniques is vital for contributing to the field and collaborating with leading medical professionals worldwide.

Here's how the RSAP x DataCamp Donates Scholarship can benefit you:
- **Master statistical analysis**: gain in-depth knowledge through a wide range of statistical analysis courses, enhancing your ability to interpret complex data and contribute to groundbreaking research.
- **Certification in R programming**: become certified in R programming, a powerful tool widely used in academic research. No prior programming experience is required, making it accessible to all medical professionals.
- **Extensive course access**: enjoy free access to 400+ courses, including those focused on data science, machine learning, and AI.
- **Hands-on projects**: get free access to 110+ projects on data science and machine learning. These practical experiences will solidify your skills and enable you to apply them in real-world medical scenarios.
- [Learn more about DataCamp](https://www.datacamp.com)

### Top 5 Healthcare-Related DataCamp Projects to Explore
- [**Dr. Semmelweis and the Importance of Handwashing**](https://www.datacamp.com/projects/20): Reanalyze the data behind one of the most important discoveries of modern medicine: handwashing.
- [**Visualizing Inequalities in Life Expectancy**](https://www.datacamp.com/projects/166): Compare life expectancy across countries and genders with ggplot2.
- [**What is Your Heart Rate Telling You?**](https://www.datacamp.com/projects/2077): Examine the relationship between heart rate and heart disease using multiple logistic regression.
- [**Hypothesis Testing in Healthcare**](https://www.datacamp.com/projects/1792): Perform hypothesis tests to determine if the adverse effects of a pharmaceutical drug are significant!
- [**Analyzing Students' Mental Health**](https://www.datacamp.com/projects/1593): Use your data manipulation skills to perform exploratory analysis on a dataset around student mental health.

### How to apply for the RSAP x DataCamp Donates Scholarship?
- Visit www.rsap.pk/datacamp to submit your scholarship application.
- This opportunity is exclusively for **RSAP Members**. For those who haven't signed up yet, they can do so through the [RSAP Membership Form](https://www.rsap.pk/join).

- The highest-scoring applications will receive access (based on availability) to DataCamp for **up to one year**, starting in **September 2024**.
