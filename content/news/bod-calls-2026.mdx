Dear RSAP Members,

It is with great pleasure that we announce the opening of the **Call for the RSAP Board of Directors (BOD) 2026-27**.

<u>**General information about the RSAP BOD**</u>:

- The BOD serves as the managing body of RSAP and is responsible for its operations.
- Each incoming BOD member is required to present strategic or operational plans at the commencement of their term.
- All BOD members must be official RSAP members.
- No BOD member may hold more than one appointed role simultaneously within a term.
- Official RSAP Letters of Appointment and Appreciation will be provided to BOD members contingent upon satisfactory performance throughout their term.
- Please refer to the RSAP Constitutional Bylaws for more information regarding the specific BOD positions outlined below.

**Available RSAP BOD Positions:**

- _Chair of the Board_ (1)
- _Secretary-Treasurer (ST), also serving as the Liaison for National & International Affairs (LNIA)_ (1)
- _Director for Human Resources (DHR)_ (1)
- _Director for Public Relations and Communication (DPRC)_ (1)
- _Liaison for Research & Publications (LRP), also serving as the National Director_ (1)
- _Liaison for Mentorship & Education (LME), also serving as the National Director_ (1)
- _Liaison for Advocacy & Networking (LAN), also serving as the National Director_ (1)

**How to Apply:**

Please complete and submit the [Application Form](https://forms.gle/jVCqSHQiagrT1riZA). Only applications submitted via the form link above will be considered. Applications submitted via email or other platforms will be deemed invalid.

All valid applications will be reviewed and evaluated by RSAP, and shortlisted applicants will be invited for a brief interview. Applicants will be notified of the results by the end of March 2026.

**Deadline:**

All applications must be submitted via the form by **March 8, 2026, at 23:59 PKT (Pakistan Time)**. Late applications will not be considered and will be deemed invalid.
